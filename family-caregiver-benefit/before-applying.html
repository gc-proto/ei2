Step 3. What you need before you apply

Make sure you have all the information listed below to complete the application:

  • the names and addresses of your employers for the last 52 weeks
  • the dates you were employed with each employer and the reasons you are no longer employed with them
  • your detailed explanation of the facts, if you quit or have been dismissed from any job in the last 52 weeks
  • if you want to sign up for direct deposit: your banking information, including financial institution, branch (transit) number and your account number
  • your full mailing address (and your home address, if they are not the same)
  • your Social Insurance Number (SIN)
  • your mother’s maiden name
  • information about the critically ill or injured adult, including full name, date of birth and home address

After you have completed the application, you will need to provide supporting documents. See the “After you’ve applied” section for details.

When will the payments start?

There is a one-week unpaid waiting period before you start receiving benefits. If you are sharing benefits with other caregivers of the critically ill or injured adult, the waiting period will only be served by one caregiver.

If you are eligible and have submitted all required documents, you should receive the first payment about 28 days after you apply.

When will the payments end?

You will stop receiving the Benefit if:

  • the adult is no longer critically ill or injured
  • you or other caregivers have been paid the maximum 15 weeks the Benefit allows
  • you have received the maximum benefits payable to you that combines the Benefit with other types of EI benefits
  • 52 weeks have passed since the time the adult has been certified critically ill or injured
  • you have reached the end of your claim period*

*When you start a claim for any type of EI benefits, your claim is open for a certain period of time, usually 52 weeks. In some situations, the claim period may be extended to 2 years.

You can apply for the Benefit online by clicking “I’m ready to apply” at the bottom of this page, or in person at your local Service Canada Centre.
